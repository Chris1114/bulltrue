Since the dawn of man, human beings have been natural deceivers. It started with hunting. There is no greater deception than fooling something into thinking you are not there so you can kill it. We evolved from there. This ability to deceive is alive and well today, hanging in there better than our vanishing pinky toes. It is estimated that people lie up to 3 times in a 10-minute conversation.

Suprisingly, we are also pre-programmed by our ancestors to believe what we are told. These two forces grind against each other, making the ability to detect lies the survival skill. BullTrue is designed to test that ability...

HOW TO PLAY:
You will be presented with 10 incredible FACTS. Some are BULL, some are TRUE. The brown half of your screen is the BULL button, the green half is the TRUE button. Push a button twice to commit to your answer.

How good are your lie detection skills? Play BullTrue to find out...
