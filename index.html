Humans are natural liars. It started with hunting. There is no greater deception than fooling something so you can kill it. These days, it is estimated that people lie up to 3 times in a 10-minute conversation.

We are also pre-programmed to believe what we are told. These two forces grind against each other, making the ability to detect lies the new survival skill. BullTrue is designed to test that ability.

HOW TO PLAY:
You will be presented with 10 incredible FACTS. Some are BULL, some are TRUE. Tap or click anywhere on the left half of the screen to select BULL, use the right half to select TRUE. At this point you can change your mind or you can commit your answer by tap/clicking the same side of the screen again.

How good are your lie detection skills? Play BullTrue to find out...
